HostelHub

A dual-role mobile app designed to simplify PG management for owners and enable transparent, real-time PG discovery for hostellers.

Project Overview

HostelHub replaces manual PG management and unreliable discovery methods with a structured, visual, and role-based digital experience.

Problem Statement

PG owners track occupancy using notebooks or spreadsheets, while hostellers rely on brokers or outdated listings. This causes confusion, lack of trust, and inefficiency for both sides.

User Personas

🔑

PG Owner

  • Manages multiple rooms
  • Needs quick occupancy overview
  • Wants control over tenants
🧳

Hosteller

  • New to the city
  • Needs real-time availability
  • Wants transparent pricing

Solution

A visual room-grid system for PG owners combined with location-based PG discovery for hostellers, connected through a secure approval workflow.

Key Design Decisions

Room Grid

Matches real-world mental model of floors and rooms.

Color Coding

Red, yellow, green indicate occupancy instantly.

Approval Flow

Builds trust and prevents unauthorized entry.

Edge Cases Considered

  • Location permission denied
  • Room filled during request
  • Owner rejects request
  • Upload failure

Future Enhancements

  • Online rent payments
  • Maintenance tickets
  • Chat between owner and tenant
  • Ratings & analytics
